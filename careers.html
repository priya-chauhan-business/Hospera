These are not aspirational suggestions. Every role listed here is directly tied to a specific module in the Hospera program. If you complete the course and pass the assessments, you are trained and ready for these positions.

Front Office

Front Office Supervisor / Agent

The primary target role for Hospera graduates. Handle check-in, check-out, guest queries, Opera PMS, and shift-level responsibility — trained across 4 months of dedicated front desk sessions including graded simulation tests.

India: ₹20,000 – ₹35,000 / month

Phone-based reservations, Opera booking workflows, availability management, upselling, and email confirmation — all trained in live call simulations with instructor scoring throughout the program.

Placement Support

Exactly what Hospera does to get you hired

Placement support at Hospera is not a vague promise. It is a structured, six-step process built into the final month — so by the time you graduate, your job search is already in motion.

01

CV & LinkedIn Profile

Your CV is written and formatted specifically for luxury hotel recruitment. Your LinkedIn is built to attract hotel HR teams with the right headline, about section, and skills that surface in recruiter searches.

02

Cover Letter Coaching

You learn to write a compelling, personalised cover letter for each hotel brand — with the tone, structure, and language that premium hotel recruiters actually respond to.

03

Mock Interview Round 1

A formal HR-style panel interview, video recorded. You receive written feedback on communication, grooming, confidence, and clarity — and you improve before Round 2.

04

Mock Interview Round 2

A competency-based panel interview with Opera knowledge questions, situational scenarios, and an Arabic greeting to the panel. The toughest preparation before the real hotel interview.

05

Hotel Referral Letters

Every graduate receives a personal reference letter from the founding directors — London hotel professionals with MSc qualifications. These carry genuine weight with hotel HR teams in India and the Gulf.

06

Direct HR Introductions

Where possible, Hospera introduces graduates directly to hotel HR contacts. You are not applying cold — you arrive with a warm introduction from people who have worked inside these environments.

Common Questions

What students ask before they apply

Do I need a hospitality background to join?
No. The program starts from the very beginning. Students with no prior experience are welcome — Month 1 is specifically designed to build your foundation from scratch.
What if I don't know any Arabic?
Arabic is taught completely from scratch as part of the program. No prior knowledge is needed. By Month 5 you will be able to conduct a full hotel interaction in Arabic.
Is placement guaranteed?
Hospera provides 100% placement assistance — structured CV support, referrals, and hotel introductions. Outcomes depend on student performance, but every graduate receives active support until placed.
Can I apply for Gulf roles from Hyderabad?
Yes. Hospera includes Gulf-specific preparation throughout the program — Arabic, UK service standards, Opera PMS — and provides guidance on Gulf application processes after graduation.
