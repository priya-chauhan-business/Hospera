About Hospera

More Than
An Institute

Hospera Institute is a UK-founded hospitality training institute based in Hyderabad, India. We do not just teach hotel operations. We transform ambitious individuals into industry-ready professionals.

Our program combines global standards with placement opportunities, ensuring graduates have the skills, confidence, and connections to launch successful careers in luxury hospitality.

Course Timeline

Six months to career readiness

From foundation skills to placement preparation — a structured journey through every discipline of luxury hospitality.

Month 1

Foundation

Hotel etiquette, communication, grooming, and service behavior basics.

Month 2

Front Office Basics

Reservations, desk flow, guest arrival handling, and check-in confidence.

Month 3

Opera PMS

Billing routines, room allocation, guest profiles, and software confidence.

Month 4

Housekeeping

Room setup, quality inspection, presentation detail, and VIP standards.

Month 5

Arabic & Service

Guest phrases, Gulf hospitality language, and service refinement practice.

Month 6

Placement Prep

Interview practice, assessments, professional polish, and employer readiness.
