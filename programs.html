Core Program

6-Month Hospitality Program

A complete short-term hotel management course in Hyderabad covering all major operational areas needed for entry-level hospitality jobs.

Month 1

Foundations & Communications

Professional mindset, communication, grooming and service basics.

Month 2

Opera PMS & Communications

Hotel systems exposure, process understanding and guest-facing communication.

Month 3

Front Office & Reservations + Arabic Foundation

Reservations, front office etiquette and Arabic basics.

Month 4

Front Office Simulations & Operations + Arabic Intermediate

Practical desk simulations, issue handling and service confidence.

Month 5

Operations & Business Skills + Arabic Advanced

Coordination, reporting, workplace etiquette and service professionalism.

Month 6

Placement Preparation

Interview preparation, resume refinement and hiring readiness.
