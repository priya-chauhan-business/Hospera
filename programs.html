Arabic Language

Conversational Arabic for Gulf hospitality

Master conversational Arabic, the language of luxury hospitality across major Middle Eastern destinations. The module is designed to support high-paying hotel opportunities in the Gulf region.

Phase 1

Beginner

Months 1-2: greetings, introductions, numbers, hotel vocabulary, and basic guest-facing confidence.

Phase 2

Intermediate

Months 3-4: service language, complaint handling, and food and beverage terminology.

Phase 3

Advanced

Months 5-6: VIP guest interaction, cultural awareness, and business communication.

Why Learn Arabic?
  • Higher earning potential in Gulf region hospitality roles
  • Access to Saudi Arabia’s growing tourism and hotel expansion
  • Continued demand across Qatar, UAE, and the wider Gulf market
  • Demand for service-ready talent exceeds supply in many hotel positions
