Programs

Hospitality course curriculum designed for practical careers.

This page explains the full 6-month hospitality program, detailed course structure, and the exact skills students develop.

6-Month Hospitality Program

One flagship program for students entering hospitality careers.

Hospera offers one focused hospitality program in Hyderabad to build service confidence, operational skills, and interview readiness.

Front Office

Guest Handling and Reception Basics

Learn check-in workflow, reception etiquette, guest communication, and service presentation standards.

F&B Service

Dining Service and Guest Experience

Develop food and beverage service confidence, table etiquette, and coordinated guest-facing delivery.

Housekeeping

Room Standards and Operations Discipline

Understand cleanliness standards, detail orientation, and operational support in hotel environments.

Course Structure

Monthly learning roadmap for progressive development

Students move from fundamentals to career readiness through a clear month-by-month structure.

Month 1

Hospitality Basics

Professional behaviour, service attitude, and hospitality workplace fundamentals.

Month 2

Communication Training

Guest communication, confidence practice, and workplace conversation standards.

Month 3

Front Office Module

Reception operations, guest greeting protocol, and reservation coordination basics.

Month 4

F&B Service Module

Restaurant service flow, guest interaction etiquette, and team coordination.

Month 5

Housekeeping Module

Room management standards, quality checks, and operations support discipline.

Month 6

Career Readiness

Interview guidance, self-introduction training, and role readiness support.

Skills You Will Learn

Practical and professional skills aligned to hospitality jobs

Course outcomes are designed around the skills employers expect in entry-level hotel roles.

Guest Service Communication

Polite and confident interaction style for front-office and customer-facing roles.

Grooming and Presentation

Workplace appearance, body language, and professionalism for hospitality environments.

Operations Awareness

Cross-functional understanding of front office, F&B, and housekeeping workflows.

Service Discipline

Attention to detail, punctuality, and quality standards expected in hotel operations.

Interview Readiness

Confidence-building for interviews, role introductions, and professional communication.

Career Foundation

Preparedness for entry-level roles in hotels, guest service teams, and hospitality operations.
