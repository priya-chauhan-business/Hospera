Course Structure

How the 6-month hospitality training is organized

Each month introduces practical concepts that build toward confident, role-ready performance.

Month 1

Foundation

Hospitality basics, professionalism, and service mindset.

Month 2

Communication

Guest interaction, spoken clarity, and confidence.

Month 3

Front Office

Reception operations and first-impression standards.

Month 4

F&B Service

Table service flow and hospitality etiquette.

Month 5

Housekeeping

Quality standards and operational discipline.

Month 6

Career Prep

Interview practice and workplace readiness.
