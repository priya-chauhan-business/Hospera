About Hospera

More Than a Course

Hospera Institute is a UK-founded hospitality training institute based in Hyderabad, India. We do not just teach hotel operations. We transform ambitious individuals into industry-ready professionals.

Our program combines global standards with placement opportunities, ensuring graduates have the skills, confidence, and connections to launch successful careers in luxury hospitality.

Industry Recognized

UK-Designed Curriculum

A premium training structure shaped around hospitality roles, software practice, service culture, and placement readiness.

Expert Faculty

Trained by hotel professionals

Students learn from mentors who understand luxury hotel standards, guest expectations, and real-world operational pressure.

Global Standards

Built on international hotel expectations

We prepare students to meet global hospitality requirements through real-world systems, communication, and service excellence.

Signature Modules

Four modules that shape real hotel readiness

Hospera combines systems, language, simulations, and management thinking so students develop both operational confidence and professional judgement before placements begin.

Core System

Opera PMS Training

Master the industry-standard hotel management system used in international properties. Learn reservations, guest profiles, billing processes, and front office operations through hands-on practice.

Gulf Readiness

Arabic Language

Develop practical Arabic communication skills tailored for Gulf hospitality. Learn essential phrases, guest interaction language, and workplace communication used in UAE and Middle East hotels.

Live Practice

Interactive Learning

Train through real hotel simulations, role plays, and live scenarios. Build confidence, grooming standards, and service etiquette required to perform in a professional hotel environment.

Advanced Insight

Masters-Inspired Training

Gain advanced business understanding through concepts like SWOT analysis, Mendelow's Matrix, and guest psychology (colour preferences) to enhance decision-making and service excellence.

Course Timeline

Six months of intensive training, from foundation skills to placement preparation.

Month 1

Foundation

Hotel etiquette, communication, grooming, and service behavior basics.

Month 2

Front Office Basics

Reservations, desk flow, guest arrival handling, and check-in confidence.

Month 3

Opera PMS

Billing routines, room allocation, guest profiles, and software confidence.

Month 4

Housekeeping

Room setup, quality inspection, presentation detail, and VIP standards.

Month 5

Arabic and Service

Guest phrases, Gulf hospitality language, and service refinement practice.

Month 6

Placement Prep

Interview practice, assessments, professional polish, and employer readiness.
