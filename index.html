6-Month Hospitality Program

Course Structure

A practical, step-by-step hospitality training program covering hotel operations, guest interaction, systems, business skills, Arabic support, and interview preparation.

Month 1

Foundations & Communications

Hospitality basics, grooming, service culture, workplace behavior, and communication fundamentals.

Month 2

Opera PMS & Communications

Introduction to hotel systems, Opera PMS awareness, guest handling, and professional communication.

Month 3

Front Office & Reservations + Arabic Foundation

Reception workflows, booking processes, guest interaction, and Arabic basics for hospitality contexts.

Month 4

Front Office Simulations & Operations + Arabic Intermediate

Hands-on roleplay, desk simulations, shift operations, and improved Arabic communication support.

Month 5

Operations & Business Skills + Arabic Advanced

Hotel coordination, workplace professionalism, reporting, service standards, and advanced Arabic practice.

Month 6

Placement Preparation

CV building, interview preparation, confidence coaching, and readiness for recruitment partner interviews.
